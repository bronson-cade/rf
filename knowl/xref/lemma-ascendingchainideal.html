Lemma 3.2.10.

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(I_1 \subseteq I_2\subseteq I_3\subseteq \cdots \) an ascending chain of ideals. Then
\begin{equation*} I = \bigcup\limits_{j=1}^\infty I_j \end{equation*}
is an ideal.
