Corollary 1.2.6. The Division Algorithm for \(\Z\).

Let \(a,b\in \Z\) with \(b\ne 0\text{.}\) Then there exist unique integers \(q,r\) such that \(a = bq + r\text{,}\) where \(0 \le r \lt |b|\text{.}\)
